About Ninex

Ninex LLC, headquartered in Westfield, New Jersey, is a small options-trading firm with a focus on day-trading
and the implementation of low-risk, consistently profitable strategies. Established as a formal business entity
in December 2022, our portfolio's roots extend back to March 2020. Through a diligent and strategic approach,
we reliably deliver growth and profitability to accounts under our management. Every trade is meticulously
calculated, a testament to our expertise and commitment to excellence in the dynamic world of options trading.
In addition to core operations, Ninex takes pride in fostering financial literacy and skill development, offering
guidance and resources for aspiring young traders.

Beginning

The Ninex portfolio had humble beginnings, taking root in March 2020 within the confines of a bedroom. Armed with determination and a modest four-figure account, we began experimenting with basic trades. Through meticulous research, strategic decision-making, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge, a small-scale initiative was transformed into the foundation of what is now Ninex LLC. This grassroots origin story reflects not only the entrepreneurial spirit of our team but also underscores the potential for growth within the trading world.

Expanding

In December 2022, after growing the original investment by over 150%, Ninex was officially registered as an LLC. Through the first year of operations, being managed almost entirely by college students, Ninex's accounts more than tripled in value. May 2022 marked the placement of our first five-figure trade, highlighting the company's rapid growth and the efficacy of our approach to navigating the options market.

Improving

Ninex's progress thus far is a testament to the entrepreneurial acumen driving our journey in the dynamic realm of trading. Ninex continues to achieve portfolio growth and expansion through a strategic mix of trading approaches, including credit spreads, iron condors, and butterfly spreads. Our limited-risk, short-term strategies are meticulously executed to capitalize on market movements while mitigating potential losses. The company remains agile, adapting to market conditions and refining our strategies based on real-time analysis. Ninex is continously working on the development of more sophisticated algorithms and innovative strategies, incorporating quantitative models to enhance decision-making processes and further optimize trade execution.
