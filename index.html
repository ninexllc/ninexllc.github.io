About Ninex

Ninex LLC is a small hedgefund with a focus on day-trading and the implementation of low-risk,
consistently profitable strategies. Established as a formal business entity in December 2022, our portfolio's
roots extend back to March 2020. Through a diligent and strategic approach, we reliably deliver growth and
profitability to accounts under our management. Every trade is meticulously calculated, with fixed risks
and consistent short-term profits.

Beginning

The Ninex portfolio had humble beginnings, emerging as a sub $1000 account in March 2020. Through tedious research, consistency, and a series of trial and error, the account started to grow. Although gains at first were minimal, with most trades not even breaking $100, profits were consistent and stress was tolerable. Returning roughly 300% over the course of two years, a small-scale initiative was transformed into the foundation of what is now Ninex LLC.

Expanding

In December 2022, after the original investment had accumulated real value, Ninex was officially registered as an LLC. Through the first year of operations, Ninex recieved $20,000+ from investors. From January 2023 to December 2023, our accounts returned a total of 283%.

Improving

Ninex's progress thus far is a testament to the entrepreneurial acumen which this company was built on. As we continue to grow, new opportunities arise and improvements are constantly being made. Not only are we on track for another profitable year, but we are also providing a way for aspiring traders to produce similar results.
